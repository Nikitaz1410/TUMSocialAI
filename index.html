About TUM Social AI

Germany's first student initiative combining AI innovation with social impact

Who We Are

At TUM, we noticed a gap: while AI is taking off everywhere, it's not yet being effectively used to solve the kinds of humanitarian and environmental problems that truly matter. We have thriving clubs for AI technology, social projects, and startups in Munich, but no community that brings all three together.

That's where TUM Social AI comes in. We founded Social AI to fill this void – combining cutting-edge AI innovation with social and environmental good, powered by an entrepreneurial spirit. We're here to make sure that AI isn't just used for corporate profit, but also to help those who need it most.

Our Mission & Vision

Creating meaningful change through technology

Mission

To provide a collaborative A-Team where ambitious students can harness AI technology to create solutions for humanitarian and environmental challenges. Every day, we work towards turning ideas into impactful AI projects – from concept to prototype – that make a positive difference in the world around us.

Vision

To become Germany's leading student organization for AI-driven social innovation, demonstrating that AI can be a force for good. We envision a future where AI is not only about tech giants and commercial use-cases, but equally about empowering nonprofits, aiding communities, and protecting our planet. In short, we want to ensure that AI's incredible power benefits everyone, especially those who need it the most.

Our Projects and non-profit Partners

Ongoing initiatives with our partners

Gender Bias in LLMs

We're developing a systematic evaluation framework that uncovers how gender cues influence the behavior of major large language models. By sending controlled prompt templates to several commercial LLMs and scoring their responses with clear quantitative indicators, we identify where biased patterns emerge across different areas.

LLMs Gender Bias Evaluation

Funding Matching

We designed a platform to pair valuable cultural and social projects with the funding they deserve. We run a large database of German funding organizations and developed a clever algorithm to handle the matchmaking. This system guarantees that truly good ideas always find the financial support they need to succeed.

Database Matching Funding

Water Sources in Africa

We're building a Machine Learning system to predict where new groundwater sources are likely to be found across Africa. Using satellite data and remote sensing, we create a detailed mapping pipeline. This approach aids our NGO partners in accessing fresh water, supporting millions of people who urgently need this fundamental resource.

Computer Vision Africa Water

Optimizing NGO FinOps

We're collaborating with the Spanish NGO Entreculturas to make their critical operations smarter. Our AI system automatically handles invoice compliance checks and uses an algorithm for grant matching, effectively linking projects to funding. This helps Entreculturas work faster, cut down on errors, and unlock essential money to expand education and social opportunities worldwide.

Compliance Invoices Grants

Integration Support

Integration can be tough, especially with language barriers. We're prototyping a RAG-based Chatbot for an NGO that offers social support and language courses. The system uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to pull and synthesize complex information from their site naturally. It provides instant, understandable answers, helping migrants quickly find the specific support they need.

Social integration Chatbot Website

A Club for Students to Grow

Social AI isn't your typical "meet-and-greet" student club – it's a hands-on, high-energy environment for personal growth and real impact

If you're an ambitious student willing to roll up your sleeves, this is the place to supercharge your learning. Be ready to spend at least 10 hours per week with us: brainstorming, coding, and creating something meaningful. It's a significant commitment, but the rewards make it worthwhile.

Real-World Projects

You will work on projects that have tangible outcomes. From day one, you'll be applying AI to solve challenges faced by NGOs or communities. You'll experience the entire process from identifying needs with partner organizations to designing and implementing game-changing AI solutions.

Mentorship & Learning

We connect you with industry experts, researchers, and PhD students in AI and related fields. Through one-on-one mentoring and group sessions, you'll get guidance on technical skills and advice on project development.

Personal Growth

This is a place to challenge yourself. You might lead a project team, learn to pitch your ideas, or manage collaboration between technical and non-technical members. You'll develop leadership, teamwork, and project management skills.

For NGO Partners – AI Support for Your Mission

Are you an NGO facing challenges in fundraising, outreach, or your operative day-to-day?

TUM Social AI is eager to collaborate with mission-driven organizations to co-create AI solutions tailored to your needs.

Why collaborate with us?

  • Social Impact Collaboration: We put a dedicated student AI team on your problem statement. If we identify a problem we can solve through AI/ML we will proceed with developing a custom AI solution together.
  • Tech Ally at Zero Cost: As an NGO partner, you don't pay us. Your contribution is your expertise and time. We handle the technology side, so you can focus on your mission.
  • Innovative, Implementable Tools: Projects are built with your operational reality in mind. The goal is not just research, but practical tools that your organization can actually use on the ground.
  • Amplified Impact: By leveraging AI, you can multiply your reach, efficiency, and effectiveness without needing to expand your staff or budget.

Requirements for NGO Partners

  • Mission fit: A real-world problem where AI can help, aligned with your impact goals.
  • Data readiness: Usable data (or a clear path to access appropriate open data).
  • Collaboration bandwidth: A point person and timely feedback during discovery and pilot.
  • Responsible use: Privacy respected and ethical considerations acknowledged.
  • Continuity: A simple plan for who owns and uses the solution after handover.

Interested in partnering up as an NGO?

We'd love to hear from you. Contact us to discuss your challenges and explore how our students can support your work.
